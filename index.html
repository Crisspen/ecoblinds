    F.A.Q

    Why window blinds over curtains?

    • Control the light entering the room

      window blinds provide you with more control over the amount of light entering the room, you can cover the entire window or just part of the window according to your own needs e.g the slats on some window blinds are so fitted tightly together that they do not allow any light in at all.

    • Countless styles, colours & patterns

      Window blinds come in an endless variety of styles, colours & patterns allowing you to create the perfect look in your office, kitchen, living room or bathroom. Choose from mini blinds, vertical blinds, roman blinds and even cordless blinds which are safer for children & pets as well as being elegant.

    • Wide variety of materials

      The variety of materials that blinds come in allows you to choose the blind that is most suitable for you in terms of both price and style e.g if your office or living room contains wooden furniture you can complement the room by installing wooden blinds. You can also have plastic blinds, bamboo blinds, aluminium blinds and other materials to choose from to ensure that you can always find the right blinds for any room in your office or home.

    • Enjoy complete privacy

      Blinds provide you with a level of privacy that you cannot get from many other options such as curtains. For the ultimate privacy control consider installing top-down shades. Using these you can lower the shade from the top so that the natural light can come in but you can still enjoy as much privacy as you require.

    • Easy to maintain

      Finally one of the best things about window blinds is that they are typically very easy to maintain. In fact amny blinds simply require a quick wipe with a damp cloth from time to time to remove the dust. Compare this to curtains which need to be washed once in a while and blinds start to look like the most convinient option. In addition they are also long lasting especially blinds made from woodor aluminium meaning you won't have to replace them until you decide you are ready for a change.
